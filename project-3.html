Tend

Tend offers interesting people a fun, customizable, eco-conscious phone option that empowers you to make kind, environmentally conscious choices. Tend's tech is designed with the planet in mind and serves each user's unique needs.

Ads showcasing how the phone fits into real, evolving lives. Each story reflects the message: built for change, made to last. Reflecting how the phone is built for the change in your life, and for revolutionary change in the world.

Van Life

Van-life ad: Personalizing and upgrading over time – reflects adaptability and durability.

One of a kind

Gaining meaning through use – reflects customizable, lasting tech.

Stuck together

Long-term love vs. temporary – emphasizes commitment and longevity.

Rationale

These ads strategically connect with the target audience in multiple ways:

Through format, I chose short, engaging YouTube ads, keeping in mind that younger audiences require more engagement to stay focused. YouTube is particularly effective because viewers cannot skip the ad until 30 seconds in, allowing time to capture their attention.

Through content, they tap into themes of exploration, the outdoors, self-expression, friendship, and meaningful relationships—themes that resonate deeply with younger audiences focused on experimentation and change.

Through tone, the ads strike a balance between hopefulness, high energy, and a touch of rebellion, encouraging viewers to imagine a better, freer future and inspiring them to be part of the change.
